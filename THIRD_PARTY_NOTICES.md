# Third-Party Notices

## Dataset

This model was trained from the Kaggle-hosted **PCB Defect dataset**:

- <https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/norbertelter/pcb-defect-dataset>

The six-defect taxonomy is associated with:

- Weibo Huang and Peng Wei, *A PCB Dataset for Defects Detection and Classification*
- <https://arxiv.org/abs/1901.08204>
- <https://github.com/Ixiaohuihuihui/Tiny-Defect-Detection-for-PCB>
